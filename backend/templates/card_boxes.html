Characters

Helen Stoner: Fears for her life, haunted by her sister's death.
Dr. Grimesby Roylott: Violent, controlling, and interested in Indian wildlife.
Julia Stoner: Twin sister to Helen Stoner, died mysteriously two years prior.
Mrs. Hudson: Housekeeper at 221B Baker Street
Honoria Westphail: Aunt to Helen and Julia Stoner.
Percy Armitage: Fiancee of Helen Stoner, the second son of Mr. Armitage.

Clues

The Bell-Rope: A seemingly functional bell-rope hangs beside Helen Stoner's bed, the tassel resting on the pillow. However, closer inspection reveals it's a dummy, not connected to any bell or wire. It's fastened to a hook directly above a small ventilator opening into Dr. Roylott's room.
The Ventilator: A small ventilator opening connects Helen Stoner's room to Dr. Roylott's. It's a recent addition to the house. It is far too small for an adult to pass through.
Dr. Roylott's Menagerie: Dr. Roylott keeps exotic animals, including a cheetah and a baboon, which roam the grounds. A saucer of milk sits atop his safe. Neighbors fear him and his animals.
The Will: Mrs. Stoner\u2019s will stipulates that her daughters receive \u00a3250 annually upon marriage. The estate's income has dwindled, making two marriages financially crippling.
The Curled Dog Lash: A dog lash is found in Dr. Roylott\u2019s room, curled and tied into a loop. Its purpose is unclear.
Gypsy Encampment: A band of Gypsies are allowed to camp on the Roylott's land. Helen connects them to the mention of \u201cband\u201d but this connection is tenuous at best.
The Clamped Bed: Helen Stoner's bed is bolted to the floor and cannot be moved. This fixes its position relative to the ventilator and the bell-rope. {% endblock %}
